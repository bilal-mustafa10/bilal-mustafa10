Hello, I'm a Computer Science graduate from Cardiff University. My tenure as a Software Engineer at CloudAvian taught me the intricate process of full-stack development, reinforced the importance of user-focused design, and highlighted the role of effective teamwork in successful software projects.

I supplemented my academic learning with self-driven projects, including 'Mindmate', a well-being app for university students, and a job application tracker. These projects allowed me to delve deeper into real-world applications of my technical skills and gave me insights into problem-solving and project management.

Beyond work and study, I also ventured into the competitive world of hackathons. I took part in the TFW Hackathon and Cardiff NLP Hackathon, where I worked on interesting projects such as an AI-powered chatbot for snack dataset analysis and a feature to combat anti-social behaviour for the TFW app. These experiences gave me the opportunity to innovate, collaborate, and bring meaningful solutions to life under tight deadlines.

I'm excited to learn, contribute, and create a meaningful impact in the world of software development.
